Both datasets show interesting temporal patterns. Coffee prices and developer activity demonstrate similar seasonal patterns with peaks in mid-year and troughs at the beginning and end of the year.

While these datasets are completely unrelated in causation, the temporal alignment reveals an intriguing coincidence. This visualization serves as a reminder that correlation does not imply causation, and that temporal patterns can emerge independently across disparate domains.

Note: This dashboard fetches real data from public APIs. The relationship between coffee prices and developer productivity is purely coincidental and should not be interpreted as evidence of any causal relationship.
